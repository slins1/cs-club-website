Salary Information

As per the 2014 agreement with the Faculty/Staff Union, the rate you pay for your permit depends on your salary level. A combined day and evening permit for a faculy/staff member earning less than $20,000 per year costs $15/year, for example, whereas the same permit for a faculty/staff member earning greater than $80,000 is $45/year.
